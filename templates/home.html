Informatics Careers

Informatics Careers is a community-driven learning platform for data science and machine learning. Students can take online courses, build real-world projects and interact with a global community. They will be able to learn practical skills from Silicon Valley engineers, build real-world portfolio projects, and earn a certification. Students can kick start their data science careers.

Our sincere hope is to empower students to program more, learn more, and share more. Therefore, we introduce to them the Informatics Careers Contributor Program where they will get an exciting opportunity to publish their work (blogs and Jupyter notebooks) on our platform and share it with our 25,000+ global data science community members from 180+ countries.
